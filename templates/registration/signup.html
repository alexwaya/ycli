By registering with the ASF forum, stakeholders join the county chapters of their county where they have active programs.

This enables them to jointly take part in advancing the mandate of the ASF which involves the following key areas of action:

1.) To strengthen citizen engagement processes and practices.
2.) To enhance accountability and transparency in governance through strengthening the capacity of citizen led social audit and accountability processes.
3.) To coordinate and collaborate.
4.) Networking and Coordination, Joint action, Awareness Raising and Debate .
